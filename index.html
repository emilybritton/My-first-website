Why Jojo's Bizarre Adventure is the Best Anime

Below you will find reasons on why JJBA is the best anime and should be watched.

The Plot

Every season feels like a new show but they are all connected together through a family tree. First season was about vampires, second season was about Gods, third season had big, strong men that had super powers from tarot cards, season four is about a murder mystery, season five is about Italian mafia, and six is based in prison.

There are more manga's with different plots for each book, but those have not been made into shows yet. The newest one has been adapted as a Netflix original that will be coming out at the end of this year. The girl, Jolyne, is the daughter of the main character from season three.

Each season has the protagonist as a family of the Joestar line, and their first name is always Jo, hence why the show is called Jojo's Bizarre Adventure.

Characters

Below are the main Jojo characters starring in each season, starting with season one and moving to season six.

  • Jonathan Joestar
  • Joseph Joestar
  • Jotaro Kujo
  • Josuke Higashikata
  • Giorno Giovanna
  • Jolyne Cujoh
